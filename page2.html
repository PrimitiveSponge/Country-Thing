Germany's cars

Now, Germany is known for their cars, for the better or for the worse. Here are some of their top cars.
1.) BMW: Bayerische Motoren Werke AG, abbreviated as BMW, is a German multinational manufacturer of
performance luxury vehicles and motorcycles headquartered in Munich, Bavaria, Germany.

2.) Audi: Audi AG Aktiengesellschaft, commonly referred to as Audi, is a German automotive manufacturer
of luxury vehicles headquartered in Ingolstadt, Bavaria, Germany.

3.) Volkswagon: Volkswagen, abbreviated as VW, is a German motor vehicle manufacturer
headquartered in Wolfsburg, Lower Saxony, Germany.
