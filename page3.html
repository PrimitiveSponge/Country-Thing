Places to visit

Here are a few iconic places to visit.
1.) Berlin: You can visit places like the Brandenburg Gate, the Reichstag Building, and the East Side Gallery.

2.) Munich: You can visit places like the English Garden, the Nymphenburg Palace, and the Marienplatz.

3.) Frankfurt: You can visit places like the Stadel Museum, the Main Tower, and the Palmengarden.
